Our Mission

Empower students and graduates with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to make informed and successful career choices in diverse and exciting field of science. We are dedicated to nurturing the scientific talent of our students and helping them embark on fulfilling and impactful careers.

Our Vision

Become a premier center for career development, equipping students with skills, oppotunities, and guidance ti excel in the global job market and positively influence society.

Academic Counselling

Our team of experienced academic counsellors provides one-on-one guidance to students, helping them explore their academic interests and goals within the field of science.

Skills Development

We offer workshops, seminars and training programs to enhance essential skills such as resume writing, interview preparation, communication skills, etc.

Networking Opportunities

We facilitate connection between students and professionals in various scienfic industries, helping students build valuble networks for their feture careers.

Events and Workshops

We organize career fairs, panel discussions, and industry-specific events to expose students to a wide range of career options in science.
